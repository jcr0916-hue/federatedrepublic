Eligibility Confirmation
The Elections Panel confirms that the candidate meets all constitutional eligibility requirements for the office of Legat Consul under §2.4 as of the date of filing. Eligibility verification is current. No disqualifying conditions identified.
Candidate Declaration
I, Ines Carrow, hereby file notice of candidacy for the office of Legat Consul in the Year 13 federal electoral period pursuant to §7.1 of the Constitution of the Federated Republic.

Campaign finance registration is filed separately under §8.1. This notice does not constitute a campaign communication under §8.1 and is not subject to the electoral finance disclosure requirements applicable to campaign materials.

I meet all eligibility requirements for the office as confirmed by the Elections Panel above. I affirm that this notice is accurate and complete as of the date of filing.

The Sunderland file is not closed.
Elections Panel Note
This record is published to the NRS upon receipt of a complete and verified candidacy filing. Publication constitutes the formal NRS record of candidacy. This record does not constitute an endorsement of the candidate by the Elections Panel or any other constitutional body.
