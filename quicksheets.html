Quick Sheets

Twelve plain-language visual guides to how the constitution actually works — one page apiece. Each distills a whole area of the document into what you'd want on a single sheet: the dual executive, the courts, the Monitors, elections, rights, the budget, and more.

These are companions to the Annotated Edition — where each sheet is also linked from the article it summarizes — and to the Scenario Library, which shows the same mechanisms under pressure. Every sheet is printable and self-contained.
