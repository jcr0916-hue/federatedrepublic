Presidential Trade Authority · §2.1.i · Adversarial Concurrence Dispute
The Concurrence Dispute
Year 28. President Solis concludes the Valdenmere Digital Services Agreement — a national treatment compact for digital services — without PM concurrence, believing it falls entirely within the presidential trade domain. PM Adara Vehn publishes her disagreement to the NRS within two days. The SC rules 5-4 within five days: national treatment obligations within Republic territory materially affect the domestic economic sphere; PM concurrence was required. The agreement is void, re-concluded eight days later with PM concurrence, and ratified 20 days after that. §2.1.i's dispute resolution mechanism in adversarial operation.
