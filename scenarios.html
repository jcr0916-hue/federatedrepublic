Four people. One week. Not a crisis scenario — the Republic doing what it was designed to do. A clinic intake coordinator watching an Early Warning audit update overnight. About these scenarios. These are hypothetical — the Federated Republic does not exist. The scenarios are designed to show how constitutional provisions function under real pressure, not to predict or prescribe political outcomes. Names, events, and statistics are invented for illustrative purposes. The constitutional provisions tested are drawn directly from the document.
