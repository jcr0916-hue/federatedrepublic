About these essays. The Papers are not commentary on the Constitution — they are the case for it. Each essay takes the provision most likely to generate resistance, states the strongest objection to it, and answers that objection directly. They are written to citizens, not to constitutional scholars. They assume the reader can follow an argument, evaluate a trade-off, and disagree with a conclusion.
