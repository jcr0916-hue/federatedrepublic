NRS-Y13-0233
Diplomatic Communication — Formal Inquiry to the Sunderland Interim Authority
Record Identification
NRS Reference:NRS-Y13-0233
Record Type:Diplomatic Communication — Formal Inquiry
Publishing Authority:Foreign Affairs Directorate of the Federated Republic
Filed By:Ines Carrow, Director of Foreign Affairs
Addressed To:Sunderland Interim Authority
Related Record:Legat Consul's Note of Protest, Year 13, Month 5, Day 8
Date of Transmission:Year 13, Month 5, Day 16
Date of Publication:Year 13, Month 5, Day 16
Background
On Month 5, Day 8, the Legat Consul transmitted a formal note of protest to the Sunderland Interim Authority following an armed incursion approximately 800 meters into Korda Territory. That note addressed the incursion at the level of state responsibility. It did not seek, and was not intended to seek, an accounting of which formation crossed, under whose orders, or through what chain of command. This inquiry seeks that accounting.
Communication Summary
The Foreign Affairs Directorate has transmitted a formal inquiry requesting: (1) identification of the specific formation and its parent command structure; (2) confirmation of whether the crossing was authorized at the command level identified or occurred outside the chain of command; (3) the Interim Authority's assessment of the operational reliability of that command structure going forward. The inquiry notes that a crossing directed by an identifiable authorized command and a crossing occurring outside command control carry materially different implications for the reliability of future diplomatic engagement, and that the distinction is not addressed by a note of protest alone.
This communication is transmitted by the Director of Foreign Affairs in the ordinary exercise of the Directorate's diplomatic functions. It is not a campaign communication and is not subject to the electoral finance disclosure requirements applicable to campaign materials under §8.1.

The Directorate's engagement on this matter is unaffected by the Director's status as a declared candidate for Legat Consul and will continue on its ordinary schedule regardless of the electoral calendar.

The Sunderland file is not closed.
Status
The Interim Authority has not responded as of the date of publication. The Directorate will publish the response, or the fact of continued non-response, to the NRS upon receipt or upon expiration of the standard 30-day diplomatic response window, whichever occurs first.
This record is published to the NRS under the Foreign Affairs Directorate's standing transparency practice for diplomatic communications concerning matters of active public interest. Publication does not disclose classified operational material; see NRS-Y12-3198 for the underlying redacted intelligence assessment of Sunderland's internal command structure.
