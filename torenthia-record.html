Supreme Court · SC-Y12-0091 · Decided Year 13, Month 3 · 9–3
Corindal Industrial Partners v. NRS Panel
Whether §10.1's permanence protection admits exceptions for records containing proprietary commercial information. Holding: No. The word "permanent" is not modified. The remedy for improper inclusion is a correction notice, not removal. Dissent argues the majority should have first determined whether the EM's publication was within its constitutional mandate.
