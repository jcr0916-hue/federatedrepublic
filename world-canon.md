# World Canon — Geography of the Federated Republic of Torenthia

**Single source of truth for world-content geography.** All news, NRS entries, dispatches, and
world-page copy must be consistent with this document. The map (`region-map-tier2-base.webp` +
`map-data.js`) is the visual authority; this file states the same canon in words.
Last reconciled: session 260703 (Phase 2 geography sweep).

## The Republic — position in the world

Torenthia is a coastal country on the **western seaboard of the crescent continent** (Tier 1 map,
upper-left landmass). Open sea (the **Western Sea**) lies west and southwest. The **Torenthian
Alps** run east–west along the entire northern border. **Lake Varda**, a long lake running
north-to-southeast, defines the eastern frontier. Continental land continues north (Caldris) and
south (Valedon). Torenthia is **not** a peninsula.

## Five Cities

Verentum (capital — Aldenmere) · Calden (port — Caldenmere) · Dorven (market — Orath) ·
Rhondel (lake city — Kelvant) · Ostara (ancient capital — Solara).

## Water

- **Toren River**: runs west→east along the **northern** border region, beyond the Alps; a
  jointly-administered stretch is governed by the Year 4 bilateral treaty with Caldris.
  **Torenthia is downstream of Caldris on the main stem** (Caldris holds the headwaters) but
  **upstream on the eastern tributary**, which rises in the Torenthian Alps, flows north into
  Caldris, and carries ~60% of dry-season flow — hence the dry-season minimum-flow formula and
  the mutual dependency in the treaty renewal. Supplies ~340,000 citizens in the three
  **northern** states. The western tributary allocation was settled provisionally first;
  Vael's Varek error was naming the western tributary when the live dispute is the eastern.
- **Lake Varda**: eastern frontier lake shared with Sunderland; contested administrative
  midline; crossings run west toward Torenthian shores (Norvane north, Rhovane upper-west,
  Kelvant mid-west, Korda south). Coast guard command in Rhondel; Varenne Station in Korda.
- **Western Sea**: west/southwest. Marisvia lies across it.

## Neighbors

- **Caldris** — north & northeast, beyond the Alps. Upstream power (~85M). Economic stakes in Sunderland.
- **Sunderland** — east, across and beyond Lake Varda. Fracturing, at war; source of refugee crossings; land border with Korda in the southeast.
- **Valedon** — south. The nation that almost joined (Year 18 referendum wound; §15.6 question).
- **Seronne** — southeast buffer state. Its **northwestern** corner touches Lake Varda.
- **Marisvia** — west, across the Western Sea. Maritime democracy, closest constitutional peer.
