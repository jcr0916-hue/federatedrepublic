Changelog

Every revision to the Federated Republic — what changed, why it changed, and what gap it closes. The constitution is a living document; this is its institutional memory.

About versioning. Version numbers track the constitutional document, not the website. A version increment means at least one provision was added, revised, or removed. Minor fixes — terminology corrections, cross-reference repairs, drafting errors — are included where they affect meaning. The document is available for download at each current version. Prior versions are archived in the project repository.

v178 May 2026 Prior
§10.4.f (new) — Director-Board Authority The MA Director-Board governance relationship is now constitutionally established. The Director chairs the board and holds veto authority; the board of five decides by majority vote with the Director not holding a board vote. All timelines delegated to statute. Closes the longest-standing design gap in the document.
