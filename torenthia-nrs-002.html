Declaration Text

I, Ines Carrow, declare my candidacy for the office of Legat Consul of the Federated Republic in the Year 13 federal electoral period, in accordance with §7.1 and §11.1 of the Constitution of the Federated Republic. I attest that I meet all eligibility requirements established in §2.1 and that I have reviewed and accept the candidacy obligations established by statute and by the Elections Panel's conduct standards under §8.2.

The situation in Lake Varda requires a Legat Consul who has read every Sunderland intelligence file from the past six years. I have.

Elections Panel Certification

The Elections Panel certifies that the above declaration has been received, reviewed for constitutional eligibility, and entered into the official candidacy registry for the Year 13 Legat Consul election. This record is published to the NRS pursuant to §10.1 and §11.1. The declaration is permanent and may not be altered or removed.
